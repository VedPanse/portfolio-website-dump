BancBridge Presentation

BancBridge News



BancBridge announced today that it has been selected to present at the Treasury Management Association of Chicago's 22nd Annual Windy City Summit, May 7-9, 2008. BancBridge President, Donald Craddick, will be joined by John Scully of Bank of America, Brian Mobray of Textron Inc., and Eric Newberg of FXpress Corporation in a discussion on bank connectivity.

The presentation will detail how participants have used bank websites in a unique way for automated upload/download of banking data quickly, securely and inexpensively. “The Windy City Summit is a uniquely educational venue for banks and corporations to gain leading-edge information on operational treasury productivity,” said Craddick. “In a rapidly changing cash management industry, it provides an important environment for financial colleagues to share knowledge and objective opinions.”
