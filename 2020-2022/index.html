Detecting Fake News Using Machine Learning

I worked on detecting fake news on social media using Machine Learning. Solving this problem intrigued me because it led to chaos during the COVID-19, especially in India, as unverified home remedies for tackling the virus surfaced through social media.
I have written an article for The Lokmat Times, which has over 1.8 million readers, to make people aware of this issue. This article covers almost every basic: from impacts to prevention techniques employed to solve this issue on a large scale.
