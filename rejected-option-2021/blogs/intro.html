Introductory Blog

Hello everyone, welcome to my introductory blog.



Hello everyone. My name is Ved Panse, and I am a student at Woodridge High School, Aurangabad, India. Aurangabad might seem to be an inconspicuous city to some, but let me tell you, it is known as the 'City of Gates', with about 52 gates!


I have an interminable list of what I find to be interesting. The activities that intrigue me the most include:

  • Computer Science: Oh! I love this branch so much that I want to pursue my career in it.

  • Cricket: You might already know how religiously cricket is followed in India. But let me tell you, the feeling of playing this game is magnificent.

  • Mathematics: If it isn't the good ol' Mathematics that I find interesting.


I am a very foody person too, and I get that from my family. We usually set off to try something new from the vast cuisine of India.


Talking about sports, do you know that there are more than 800 sports in the world? Well, I cannot play all of them. But, the blood of a sportsperson runs down my family. Owing to this, I have actually played 3 different sports at the district level, and 7 different sports at the school level. So, if there is any sportsperson reading this blog who might be interested to discuss sports with me, I'm always up for it. You can contact me here.
