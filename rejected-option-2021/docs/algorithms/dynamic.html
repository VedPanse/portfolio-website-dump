Dynamic Programming

Written By Ved Panse Last Modified: 30 June 2021

In this section of the documentation, we will be learning about dynamic programming.

The Problem

Imagine that you are going on a world tour. Seems pretty pleasant, right? Well, it may not be. Suppose you are permitted to take only one suitcase with you for the trip.

For airplane boarding, the maximum weight of your suitcase must be 67 pounds. So, the problem is that you will have to take items whose sum of weight should be less than 50 pounds. Also, you will have to take the maximum number of items that you can.



The Solution

Dynamic programming is the method of approaching problems. The concept of dynamic programming deals with breaking the given problems and solving all of them step-by-step.

This concept may seem complicated; but with time, it becomes easier to solve them.
