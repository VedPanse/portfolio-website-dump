Microsoft Certifies BancBridge

BancBridge News



Microsoft certified BancBridge TreasuryAgent to run on the Server 2008 platform. Testing was conducted independently by VeriTest, a testing service of Lionbridge Technologies. BancBridge, a Microsoft Certified Partner, was pleased to get the results. Kedar Panse, BancBridge's lead technology office, summarized the certification, "Our decision to commit to Microsoft technology is continually reinforced by positive news of forward strides like this."
